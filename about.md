Hi — I'm **Thirumalaiselvan K J** (most people call me Selvan), a Senior Technical Program Manager based in Chennai, India, with **17 years in IT delivery**.

## Why this site exists

Over the years I kept getting handed project types I hadn't run before — a cloud application one quarter, a data platform the next, then a GenAI feature, then a vendor implementation. Each time, the hard part wasn't learning to *build* the thing; it was knowing **what to look for and what to ask** fast enough to be useful in the first design review.

I started writing those questions, red flags, and checklists down. This site is that notebook, cleaned up and shared. It's a **personal reference curated from industry practices and hands-on delivery experience — not an official standard.** Where a page includes a *From the field* callout, that's a real (anonymized) situation I've dealt with.
