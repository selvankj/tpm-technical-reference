    About¶

    Last reviewed: 2026-07-03 · Review cadence: quarterly

    Hi — I'm Thirumalaiselvan K J (most people call me Thiru), a Senior Technical Program Manager based in Chennai, India, with 17 years in IT delivery.

    Why this site exists¶

    Over the years I kept getting handed project types I hadn't run before — a cloud application one quarter, a data platform the next, then a GenAI feature, then a vendor implementation. Each time, the hard part wasn't learning to build the thing; it was knowing what to look for and what to ask fast enough to be useful in the first design review.

    I started writing those questions, red flags, and checklists down. This site is that notebook, cleaned up and shared. It's a personal reference curated from industry practices and hands-on delivery experience — not an official standard. Where a page includes a From the field callout, that's a real (anonymized) situation I've dealt with.

    Background¶

    • Current focus: technical program management for healthcare data platforms — multi-workstream data engineering delivery on Azure (ADF, Databricks, dbt, Airflow), account management, and pre-sales/solutioning
    • Domains: healthcare & life sciences, regulated/PMO environments, client account delivery
    • Earlier: delivery and program roles across consulting and product-services organizations, including audit-supported regulated environments
    • Certifications: PMP · AWS Certified AI Practitioner · AWS Certified Generative AI Developer

    How the site is maintained¶

    Every page carries a Last reviewed date and the guide runs on a quarterly review cadence — cloud service names, compliance rules, and default patterns drift, and stale authoritative-looking guidance is worse than none. Changes land in the changelog.
